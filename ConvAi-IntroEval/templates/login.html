What is QritiQ?

QritiQ is an innovative AI-powered platform designed specifically for students to improve their HR interview skills through conversational AI technology. Our platform simulates real interview scenarios and provides comprehensive feedback to help you succeed in your career journey.

Why QritiQ?

In today's competitive job market, interview skills are crucial for landing your dream job. QritiQ addresses this challenge by providing:

Speech Recognition

Advanced speech recognition converts your spoken responses into text for analysis

AI Evaluation

Intelligent evaluation of your communication style, content, and professional presentation

Analytics

Monitor your improvement over time with detailed performance analytics

Improvement Tips

Receive constructive suggestions and tips for interview enhancement
